SH Guide

Choose the right amount of SH (Sodium Hypocloryte) for your application

Remember SH is used to remove organic stains.
Application Concentration Ratio
Asphalt Roofs Min: 3% Max: 6%
Concrete Tile Roofd Min: 4% Max: 6%
Metal Roofs (Be careful using SH) Min: 1% Max: 3%
Vinyl Siding Min: 1% Max: 2%
Stucco Siding Min: 2% Max: 3%
Concrete Pre treatment Min: 1% Max: 3%
Concrete Post treatment Min: 3% Max: 3%
Wood (Pre soak with water) Min: 1% Max: 1%
Brick Min: 2% Max: 3%
